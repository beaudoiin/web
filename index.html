    Campus Life

    A college should strive to be more than just a download of academic textbooks and dry lectures. A well-rounded expereince equips our students to be able to tackle diverse challenges and to better integrate a health balance into their life. That is why we provide services such as: counciling, sports and recrational groups, connection groups for shared interests, social studies and oprotunites, and most importantly our faculty are trained to integrate diversity to reach common goals.

    At Pelican College there are a veriety of clubs and organizations available to students.

    A veriety of Music groups are available for students to play provided instruments together, and build stronger bonds. The last friday of each month, there is a jamapalooza hosted in the audatoriam. Basketball, Soccer & Vollyball teams and are avaible to join year round! If you think you have what it takes, come on down and try out! Not very good at sports? Our campus features many training sessions to get you tryout ready! See your student life guide to find out which one is right for you! For those of you who more into literature, debates, and creative writing groups, join us in the library every morning between 7am and 8am to get your brain flowing for the day!
